# Google Business Profile Setup Guide for MJS Web Design

## Why This Matters

Google Business Profile is critical for local SEO. It puts your business on Google Maps, shows in local search results, and builds trust with potential customers searching for web design services in Los Angeles.

## Step-by-Step Setup

### 1. **Create or Claim Your Business**

- Go to [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com)
- Click "Sign in" or create a new Google account
- Click "Manage your business" for the first time
- Enter your business name: **MJS Web Design**
- Search for your business or add it manually

### 2. **Enter Your Business Details**

Fill in the following information:

**Basic Information:**

- Business Name: `MJS Web Design`
- Phone: `(424) 225-1294`
- Email: `michael@mjswebdesign.com`
- Website: `https://www.mjswebdesign.com`

**Address/Service Area:**

- Since you're a service-area business (not location-based), select:
  - ✅ "Service area business" (do NOT enter a physical address)
  - Add Service Areas: **Los Angeles, California**
  - Expanded service area (optional): Los Angeles County, Orange County, Ventura County

**Business Hours:**

- Set your availability (e.g., Mon-Fri 9am-6pm, Closed weekends)
- Can update seasonally

### 3. **Add Business Category**

Select primary category: **Web Designer** or **Web Design Agency**
Add secondary categories:

- Web Developer
- Digital Marketing Agency (if applicable)

### 4. **Write Your Business Description**

Keep it compelling and keyword-rich (250 characters max):

_Suggested text:_

> "Professional web design for Los Angeles businesses. Custom websites, landing pages, and eCommerce solutions. Fast turnaround, mobile-friendly, SEO-optimized. Get your site live in 1-2 weeks!"

### 5. **Add Photos**

Upload at least 5 high-quality photos:

1. **Logo** - Your MJS Web Design logo
2. **Workspace** - Photo of your workspace (if available)
3. **Portfolio Example 1** - Screenshot of restaurant website
4. **Portfolio Example 2** - Screenshot of handyman website
5. **Portfolio Example 3** - Screenshot of music artist website

_Note: If you don't have workspace photos, focus on logo and portfolio samples_

### 6. **Verify Your Business**

Google will verify your business by:

- **Postcard verification** (if address provided) - Takes 5-7 days
- **Phone verification** (fastest option) - Instant
- **Email verification** - Usually 24-48 hours

Choose "Phone verification" for fastest setup.

### 7. **Add More Details**

Once verified, add:

**Services Offered:**

- Custom Website Design
- Landing Page Design
- eCommerce Web Development
- Website Optimization
- Mobile-Responsive Design
- SEO Optimization

**Attributes:**

- ✅ Mobile-friendly websites
- ✅ Custom design
- ✅ Quick turnaround
- ✅ Affordable pricing
- ✅ Free consultations

### 8. **Create Service Items (Optional)**

Add specific service offerings with pricing:

1. **Landing Page** - $150
2. **Simple Website** - $300
3. **Custom Website** - $500+
4. **eCommerce Website** - $1,000+

## Q&A

**Q: Do I need a physical office address?**
A: No. Since you're service-area based, select "service area business" and list Los Angeles as your service area.

**Q: Will this help my SEO?**
A: Yes! Google Business Profile is crucial for local SEO. Businesses with complete profiles rank 70% higher in local searches.

**Q: Can I link this to my website?**
A: Yes. Add the Google Business Profile URL to your footer or contact page.

**Q: How often should I update it?**
A: Add new posts every 2-4 weeks, update photos monthly, and respond to reviews immediately.
